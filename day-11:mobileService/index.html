Our Food

Inspired ~ Creative ~ Locally Sourced

We serve tacos, burritos, nachos, fries, rice plates, and salads. All served with your choice of pork, chicken or tofu, with the option to silog it (that's add a fried egg on top, for the non tagalog speaking customer).
