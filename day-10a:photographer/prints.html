Metallic Paper

I'm willing to bet that you have never heard of metallic paper. I hadn't until a few years ago. This is a crying shame because it is stunning! What is metallic paper, you ask? Well, it's like normal paper except that it has a sheet of mylar between the paper and the emulsion. It gives a high gloss finish with intense color saturation and a sort of 3 dimensional depth to the image. This paper works especially well for aviation photography. I recommend it to all of my customers, and I've never had someone say they did not care for it.
