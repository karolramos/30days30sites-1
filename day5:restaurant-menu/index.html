Our Story

Genevieve's is a Khmer/Western restaurant in Siem Reap. We're located close to Pub Street and the Old Market. Our food is freshly source and prepared each day. At Genevieve's, we provide high quality food and genial attentive service. We are committed to giving back to the community and contributing meaninfully to the lives of young people in Siem Reap. My team and I look forward to seeing you.

~ Phil Rogers • General Manager
